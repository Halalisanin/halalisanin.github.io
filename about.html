The Person Behind It

Business owner.
Built on 13 years of doing the work.

My name is Halalisani Ngema. I run a business that serves two types of clients — people who are building their careers, and entrepreneurs who are building their companies. Both sets of clients need the same thing: someone who knows what they are doing, does it properly, and keeps them informed throughout.

My background spans 13 years across banking, HR administration, and sales. I started in HR benefits administration at Nedbank, where I spent six years processing payroll adjustments, managing employee benefits enrolments and exits, and maintaining compliance records for thousands of staff. From there I moved to the banking floor as a Multifunctional Consultant — high-volume client service, fraud prevention, ATM management, and cross-selling. Before that, I worked in telephonic sales at Metropolitan and Leza Legalwise, where I learned how to explain complex products clearly and build trust with clients I had never met in person.

That experience is what powers both wings of this business. The career services are credible because I have worked inside the hiring process. The company registration services are reliable because I understand compliance, documentation, and the importance of getting every detail right the first time.

This is not a side project. It is a professional service business — with a ticket system, dedicated inboxes, and a commitment to following up within one business day. Every client gets a reference number. Every request gets handled properly.
