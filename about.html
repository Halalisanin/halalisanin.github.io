The Person Behind It

Office professional.
Customer service veteran.

My name is Halalisani Ngema. I am an office administrator and customer service professional with more than 13 years of hands-on working experience across three industries — banking, HR administration, and retail sales.

I started my career in HR benefits administration at Nedbank, where I spent six years processing payroll adjustments, managing employee benefits enrolments and exits, handling telephonic advisory for thousands of staff, and maintaining compliance records. I was not a strategist — I was the person on the phone answering real questions and making sure the paperwork was right.

From there I moved to the banking floor as a Multifunctional Consultant — teller duties, walk-in and telephonic customer service, fraud prevention, ATM management, and cross-selling. High-volume, face-to-face, detail-oriented work every single day.

I also worked in sales at Metropolitan and Leza Legalwise, where I learned how to have real conversations with clients about financial products and legal cover. Needs-based selling, objection handling, and building client trust over the phone.

I am not a specialist. I am someone who has done the work, at every level, across a wide range of office and service environments. That practical experience is what I bring to this platform — and it is exactly what makes the advice here worth trusting.

Our Purpose

What this platform
actually does

Job sourcing & aggregation

We pull live listings from across South Africa every few hours, covering all sectors and levels — admin, customer service, banking, retail, HR, call centre, and more. One place, hundreds of listings, completely free.

Career readiness support

We help job seekers get ready before they apply. That means a CV that reads cleanly, a profile that says the right things, and interview preparation that removes the fear of the unknown.

Profile & document coaching

Most candidates are rejected before they are interviewed — because their CV does not communicate their value clearly. We fix that, drawing on 13 years of experience inside the hiring process.

Admin & clerical support

Small businesses and individuals who need professional office admin support — data capture, document preparation, correspondence, general admin — can access that service here, on demand.
