1. POPIA Information Notice

This notice is provided in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of the Republic of South Africa.

Responsible Party

Halalisani Ngema, operating this website as a personal blog and jobs aggregation board. Contact details are provided in Section 5 below.

What personal information we may process

  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us (name, email address, message content).
  • Technical data automatically collected by GitHub Pages (IP address, browser type, pages visited) — see GitHub's own privacy policy for details.
  • No registration, login, or account system exists on this website.

Purpose of processing

  • To respond to enquiries sent via the contact section.
  • To operate and improve this website.

Lawful basis

Processing is based on your consent (contacting us voluntarily) and our legitimate interest in operating a personal website.

Retention

Contact messages are retained only as long as necessary to respond. No personal data is stored in any database on this site.

Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the details in Section 5.

2. Privacy Policy

Cookies & tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising scripts. No third-party tracking pixels are embedded.

Third-party links

The Jobs Board contains links to external job listings on third-party websites (Adzuna, CareerJunction, and others). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Review their individual privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Social links

Links to LinkedIn, Facebook, GitHub, and ALX Africa are provided for your convenience. Clicking them will take you to those platforms' own environments, which have their own privacy policies.

Data security

This is a static website served over HTTPS. We do not operate servers, databases, or user accounts. There is no mechanism for us to store your data beyond what you voluntarily send via email or messaging.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Important: The Jobs Board on this website is an aggregation service only. Halalisani Ngema is not a recruiter, employment agency, or labour broker as defined under South African law.

Nature of the service

Job listings displayed on the Jobs Board are sourced automatically from third-party APIs and websites, including Adzuna and CareerJunction. The listings are presented for informational purposes to assist job seekers in finding opportunities.

No warranty on listings

  • We do not verify, endorse, or guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or current availability of any job listing.
  • Job titles, salaries, requirements, and closing dates may change or be removed by the original advertiser at any time.
  • The inclusion of a listing does not constitute an endorsement of the employer or the role.

Application process

All applications must be made directly to the original advertiser via the "Apply Now" links provided. We are not a party to any recruitment process and play no role in hiring decisions.

No fees

This Jobs Board is provided entirely free of charge to job seekers. We do not charge candidates any fees whatsoever. Be cautious of any employer asking for upfront payments — this is often a scam.

Limitation of liability

To the fullest extent permitted by South African law, Halalisani Ngema accepts no liability for any loss, damage, or harm arising from reliance on job listings displayed on this website, or from interactions with employers found through this board.

Labour law compliance

All employers listed are expected to comply with applicable South African labour legislation, including the Basic Conditions of Employment Act, the Labour Relations Act, and the Employment Equity Act. Concerns about a specific listing may be directed to us via the contact details in Section 5.
