About Me

A Practitioner
Turned Publisher

With a National Diploma in Office Management and Technology from the Durban University of Technology, I built my career inside two demanding industries: banking and HR administration. I didn't study them — I worked them, every day, for over a decade.

At Nedbank Group, I spent six years as an HR Benefits Consultant — handling the full employee benefits lifecycle for thousands of staff. Medical aid, pension, UIF, life cover, funeral policies. Payroll adjustments, audits, enrolments, exits. Not theory. Real work, real stakes, real compliance.

Before HR, I spent seven years on the banking floor as a Multifunctional Consultant — teller, customer service, fraud prevention, ATM management, technical support, and cross-selling. I know what it feels like to serve hundreds of clients a day and keep every number right.

I also sharpened my sales instincts at Metropolitan and Leza Legalwise, where needs-based selling and client advisory rounded out a profile that spans administration, service, compliance, and commercial results.

Today I combine that operational depth with growing expertise in AI tools, Claude vibe-coding, and SEO — building the digital platforms I wish had existed when I was the one looking for work.
