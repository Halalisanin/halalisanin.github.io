About Me

A Seasoned Professional

With a National Diploma in Office Management and Technology from the Durban University of Technology, I bring a comprehensive foundation in administration, HR operations, and client-facing services — built across more than a decade in the banking and financial services industry.

My career at Nedbank Group spanned over 13 years. In my HR Benefits Consultant role, I handled the full employee benefits lifecycle — onboarding, active employees, and exits — covering medical aid, pension, UIF, life cover, and funeral policies. I managed payroll adjustments, audits, enrolments, and terminations with precision, and liaised cross-functionally with HR Business Partners and Finance.

As a Multifunctional Consultant in Nedbank Retail, I delivered teller services, telephonic and walk-in customer service, fraud prevention, ATM management, first-line technical support, and cross-selling — gaining deep fluency in both retail banking operations and customer relationship management.

I also gained sales experience during stints at Metropolitan and Leza Legalwise, where I developed skills in needs-based selling, client engagement, and financial product advisory — rounding out a well-balanced profile across admin, service, and sales.

Today, I pair this operational depth with growing expertise in AI tools, SEO, and Claude vibe-coding — ready to bring modern digital fluency to any forward-thinking organisation.
