# Welcome to My Blog

Hello and welcome! My name is **Halalisani Ngema**, and I am glad you found your way here.

I built this website as part of my journey into the digital world — a journey that started with more than 13 years in HR and banking at Nedbank, and has recently taken an exciting new direction through AI certifications with ALX Africa.

## Why I Started This Blog

I wanted a space where I could share what I am learning, thinking, and experiencing as I navigate this transition. The world of work is changing fast, and I believe that people with strong people skills, operational experience, and a willingness to learn new technology have a real edge.

This blog is my way of:

- **Documenting my learning journey** — from AI tools to coding to digital content creation
- **Sharing career advice** — especially for people in HR, banking, and admin who want to grow
- **Exploring ideas** — about technology, work, and life in South Africa

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- AI tools and how to use them practically in the workplace
- HR trends and what employers are looking for right now
- My personal experiences job hunting and building this site
- Tips for anyone transitioning careers or returning to the workforce

## A Little About Me

I spent over 13 years at Nedbank — first as an **HR Benefits Consultant**, then as a **Multifunctional Consultant** in retail banking. I have helped hundreds of employees understand their benefits, and served thousands of clients across teller and service functions.

Recently, I completed two AI certifications through ALX Africa, built this website (mostly using AI tools and a bit of HTML), and have been learning Linux, coding, and digital skills.

I am based in Johannesburg, looking for opportunities in HR, administration, banking, or customer service.
